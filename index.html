üìè How to Choose Capacity

+

1.5 cu.ft (5-10 lbs): Individuals, couples, light laundry needs. Perfect for 5-7 t-shirts, underwear, and socks per load.

2.0-2.6 cu.ft (8-12 lbs): Small families (2-3 people), moderate needs. Handles towels and small blankets.

3.0-3.5 cu.ft (13-26 lbs): Larger families (4+ people). Handles comforters and heavy items.

Pro tip: Fill dryer to 50-75% capacity for optimal drying. Overloading extends time and creates damp spots.

‚ö° Understanding Wattage

+

800-900W: Slower drying (60-90+ min), lowest energy costs, gentlest on fabrics.

1000-1200W: Balanced (50-75 min), good for most users.

1300-1500W: Faster (40-60 min), closest to full-size performance.

1600-1700W: Maximum speed, highest energy use. Verify your circuits can handle it.
